**Title:** Scale-Dependent Anomalous Diffusion and Non-Stationary Lévy Flights in an Inverse Cascade

**Description:** Investigate the relationship between wavelet-filtered eddy lifetimes and Lévy flight characteristics by re-advecting tracers in velocity fields filtered at wavelet scales j ∈ {1, 2, 3, 4}. Quantify the scaling exponent δ in β(j) ∝ T_eddy(j)^δ, predicting α = 1 + β/(2−β), using the velocity snapshots coarsened to 256². Simultaneously, analyze the temporal evolution of the global energy spectrum, extracting k_peak(t) to identify the dominant wavelet scale j_peak(t) corresponding to k_peak(t) at overlapping time windows of width W = {4, 8, 12} T_L. Compare α(t) computed from tracer trajectories within each window to α(j_peak(t)) derived from wavelet-filtered advection, employing both McCulloch and Hill estimators for α to assess estimator sensitivity. Perform Augmented Dickey-Fuller and Kwiatkowski-Phillips-Schmidt-Shin tests on α(t) to determine the stationarity of Lévy flight behavior, evaluating how stationarity depends on window size W.
